Privacy Policy

Effective March 25, 2026

The short version

LogHound does not collect, store, or transmit any of your data. Everything stays on your device.

Data collection

LogHound collects no data from you. Specifically:

  • No personal information
  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No crash reports sent to us
  • No account creation required
  • No cookies or web tracking

Your log data

All logs, entries, and fields you create are stored locally on your device using Apple's on-device storage. Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export or back it up yourself.

Third-party services

LogHound does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-processing services.

Purchases are handled by Apple through the App Store. Apple's own privacy policy governs that transaction. We do not receive or store any payment information.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, notifications are scheduled locally on your device using Apple's notification system. No notification data is sent to any server.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new effective date. Given that we collect no data, we don't anticipate meaningful changes.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at loghoundsupport@gmail.com.
